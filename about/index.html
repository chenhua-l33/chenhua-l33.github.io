About

Myself

I am Chenhua Li. I’ve studied some Materials Science in the past. I switched to Computer Science. I love digital detective work. Another way to put it is that I am interested in cybersecurity. This includes the things that come along with it which are a bit niche (i.e. quantum computing). I am also drawn to things that are debatable regarding technology (i.e. HCAI).

More about Myself

I like dancing and I read. Occasionally I also do Chinese calligraphy. I am a huge fan of roguelike games including Risk of Rain and Hades. I am learning Dutch(the Belgian version) quite intensively.

I was quite a K-pop fan 3 years ago and I owned an account which has accumulated ~25k followers(I guess because of this I could write “experienced with social media related work” on my resume :P).

Intention of the blog

The other day I was reading an article talking about the digitale gekte (digital craziness in Dutch) of this era.

I myself had a very limited understanding and blurry image of the world. I struggle with attention and focus like or even more than many of us do. And as trending videos of merely 15 seconds continue to cut people’s lives (including mine) into more and more snippets and accelerate my attention span at a rate I could not even or dare to imagine before, I decided to take some time to establish a blog like this.

I want to produce some outputs to combat the digital craziness, well, at least a little bit.
