True writing has only one source: a part of life that hurts so much that you have to keep talking about it.

I didn’t keep track of how many jobs I have applied, but I always find it quite cool of other people’s visualization(usually they draw one after the offer). The fact which matters most is that you eventually get something, which means you already fought your battle and you won.

失业这件小事

失业的时候心态失衡实在是太寻常了，以至于再多提起都显得有些过于矫情了。我尝试用一种讽刺的态度对待它，每个路过的人尝试small talk问我最近在做什么我总是瞪大双眼说nothing，时常会制造一些我期待的喜剧效果。

Unemployment

I guess it is so common to have an unhealthy mindset when you are unemployed, in the sense that merely bringing the fact that “you are troubled by the unemployment” up seems too pretentious and seeking for pity. I try to cope with this situation with an ironic method of my own, anyone came across me who tried to start a small talk converstaion with “So what are you doing right now?”. I always open my eyes as wide as possible and say “Nothing” with the strongest determination, and in turn it could induce some of the comedy effect that I was hoping for.
